Poutine

Description:

Poutine is a Canadian dish featuring fries topped with cheese curds and smothered in rich gravy, creating a melty, savory comfort food experience.

Ingredients:

  • French fries
  • Cheese curds
  • Brown gravy
  1. Fry Potatoes:

    Fry sliced potatoes until they're crispy and golden brown.

  2. Prepare Gravy:

    Simmer a rich brown gravy until it's thick and flavorful.

  3. Assemble Poutine:

    Layer the hot fries with fresh cheese curds, then pour the hot gravy over the top to slightly melt the curds. Serve immediately.

  4. Mix Cheese Filling:

    In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese with an egg. Add grated mozzarella and Parmesan cheese, season with salt and pepper, and mix well.

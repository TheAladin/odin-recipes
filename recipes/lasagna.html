Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is an Italian baked dish layered with pasta sheets, meat sauce, cheese, and bechamel. It's known for its rich flavors and comforting, cheesy texture.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • tomato sauce
  • cheese
  • Egg
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Dried herbs
  1. Preheat Oven:

    Set your oven to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Boil Lasagna Noodles:

    Fill a large pot with water, bring it to a boil, add a pinch of salt. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and lay the noodles flat on a sheet of aluminum foil or a clean cloth to prevent sticking.

  3. Prepare Meat Sauce:

    In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and minced garlic; sauté until translucent. Add the ground beef or pork; cook until browned. Stir in canned tomatoes, tomato paste, salt, pepper, and dried herbs. Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes.

  4. Mix Cheese Filling:

    In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese with an egg. Add grated mozzarella and Parmesan cheese, season with salt and pepper, and mix well.

  5. Assemble Lasagna:

    Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish. Place a layer of noodles over the sauce. Spread a layer of the cheese mixture over the noodles. Repeat the layers (sauce, noodles, cheese) until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce.

  6. Add Cheese Topping:

    Sprinkle a generous layer of grated mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.

  7. Bake:

    Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.

  8. Rest:

    Let the lasagna sit for about 10-15 minutes before cutting. This helps the layers set and makes it easier to serve.

Couscous

Description:

Couscous is a North African dish made from tiny steamed balls of semolina flour, often served with a stew spooned on top. It's known for its light, fluffy texture and ability to absorb flavors from accompanying spices and broths.

Ingredients:

  • Couscous
  • Boiling water or broth
  • Salt
  • Olive oil or butter
  1. PBoil Liquid:

    Bring water or broth to a boil and add salt and a drizzle of olive oil or a dollop of butter. <

  2. Cook Couscous:

    Stir in couscous, cover the pot, and remove from heat. Let it stand for about 5 minutes.

  3. PFluff and Serve:

    Fluff the couscous with a fork to separate the grains and serve as desired.
